# Product Concept

## Status

Draft for Phase 0 planning.

## Personas

- Visitor: wants to understand what open-ai.ru offers and where to go next.
- Product owner: wants a controllable structure for publishing portal content and validating priorities.
- Contributor: wants clear product boundaries before creating content or implementation tasks.

## Jobs To Be Done

- Discover the purpose and value of the portal.
- Navigate to relevant materials, projects, or contact paths.
- Understand which parts of the portal are planned, active, or deferred.

## MVP Scope

- Public landing and navigation structure.
- Curated pages for core materials and project outputs.
- Basic content workflow that can grow without duplicating governance-heavy structure.

## User Flows

1. Visitor opens the portal and understands the offer.
2. Visitor chooses a content area or project output.
3. Visitor follows a clear call to action or contact path.

## Success Metrics

- Reviewed and accepted product concept.
- Approved MVP scope with explicit non-goals.
- First implementation backlog derived from this document.

## Roadmap

- Phase 0: approve vision, product concept, and solution concept.
- Phase 1: implement the minimum public portal.
- Phase 2: extend content, integrations, and analytics based on usage.
