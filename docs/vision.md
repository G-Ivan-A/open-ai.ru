# Vision

## Purpose

open-ai.ru is planned as a public web portal for presenting AI-assisted work, research, education materials, and practical project outputs in one coherent product experience.

## Audience

- Founder and product owner validating the portal direction.
- Contributors preparing product, content, or implementation work.
- Visitors who need a clear entry point into available AI-related materials and services.

## Product Boundaries

The repository currently stores only planning artifacts and future source-code placeholders. Detailed governance, legacy portal research, and implementation-specific contracts are intentionally outside the initial structure.

## Success Criteria

- The portal purpose is understandable from the first page.
- Product and solution concepts are reviewed before implementation starts.
- The repository remains small enough for fast review and onboarding.
