# Product Vision

open-ai.ru is intended to become a production portal for AI-assisted services, integrations, education-sync workflows, and operational AI governance.

## Target Users

- Founders and product owners who need controlled AI-assisted delivery.
- Teams adopting AI-native SDLC practices.
- Operators managing AI integrations and reusable service patterns.

## Product Principles

- Governance before scale.
- Contracts before implementation coupling.
- Human review for irreversible product and architecture decisions.
- Clear separation between production portal work and research or education materials.

## Initial Product Areas

- Authentication and profile foundations.
- Dashboard for operational visibility.
- Catalog of AI-assisted services and integrations.
- Education synchronization boundaries with the research repository.
- AI-agent contracts and review workflows.
